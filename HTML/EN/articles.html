🧬 What are steroids?

Steroids are organic compounds that have a structure made up of four linked rings of carbon. They are naturally produced in the human and animal body, but can also be synthetically manufactured for medical and athletic use.


🔍 Varieties of steroids

There are several classes of steroids including:

  • Corticosteroids: used to treat inflammation and allergies.
  • Anabolic steroids: synthetic version of testosterone, used to build muscle mass.

💪 What are the main effects of anabolic steroids?

Anabolic steroids can affect the body in a variety of ways, including:

  • Increase in muscle mass and strength.
  • Improved physical performance.
  • Metabolic changes.

⚠️ Harm and side effects

Despite the potential benefits, the use of anabolic steroids can lead to many serious side effects, such as:

  • Cardiovascular problems: increased risk of heart attack and stroke.
  • Liver problems: possible development of liver cancer.
  • Hormonal disorders: menstrual disorders, infertility, gynecomastia in men.
  • Psychological effects: aggressive behavior, depression.

📝 Conclusion

Steroids can have both medical benefits and potentially harmful health effects. Before using them, it is important to consult a doctor and consider the possible risks.
